Assortment Module

Assortment Optimization is a sales tool that optimizas the variety and quantity of good offered for sale. It involves selecting a set of products that cater to consumer preference, Market trends and brand's Identity. It also involves determining the optimal inventory and space allocation for each product based on factors like sales forecasts, inventory turnover and seasonal demand. Assortment optimization aims to maximize the revenue and profitability of the retailer.
